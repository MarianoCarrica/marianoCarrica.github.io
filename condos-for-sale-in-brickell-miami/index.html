Residences

Beauty elevates the innovative, exclusive, and sustainable features of the residences at Mercedes-Benz Places in Miami into highly desirable emotional experiences.

From studios to three-bedrooms, each residence is an immersion in modernity and luxury, meticulously conceived and created by the interiors experts at Woods Bagot, working in collaboration with Mercedes-Benz.

Settle in Above Miami

Views that call for a pause. High above the neighboring buildings, Mercedes-Benz Places in Miami provides a sky experience that’s unrivaled.

From the dark green groves of ficus in Southside Park below to the iconic white sand of South Beach, all of Miami is visible here.
