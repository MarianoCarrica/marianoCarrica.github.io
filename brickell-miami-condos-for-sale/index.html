Brickell

Neighbor­hood

Between architectural jewels and hidden gems, high-end restaurants and low-key bars, Brickell sits at the intersection of Miami’s most-coveted dining, shopping, and work-life experiences. Indulge in the luxury of city living surrounded by a distinct sense of community and sunshine, where unparalleled access to urban and natural amenities support the bustling world-class offices, hotels, and residences. The distinct skyline and cityscape create stunning day views and night city lights, while the water provides access to endless recreational activities such as boating, paddle boarding, kayaking, and more in the enviable year-round sunshine.  

Brickell is where the city meets the neighborhood and nature. Here, Mercedes-Benz Place emerges as an unparalleled and vibrant destination.
